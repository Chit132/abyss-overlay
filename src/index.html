Overlay will automatically type /who in chat for you. After joining a lobby, wait a couple of seconds before touching your keyboard to avoid interfering with autowho. [AYOR]

Game Start Notifications

Receive a notification when the game starts. Feel free to tab out during the lobby without having to worry about when the game will start!

Auto Shrink/Unshrink

Overlay will automatically switch between its shrinked and full display modes during key events so you don't miss out on any important stats changes while focusing on your game! It will shrink when your game begins and it will open back up when a player is added.

Guild Tags

Show guild tags next to player names. Makes 1 more Hypixel API call per player.
NOTE: Only applies when your own Hypixel API key is being used.

External Players

Show the stats of players who type your name in chat; these players will show up with the CALL tag. Helpful for finding parties in lobbies. Also show players who send you party and friend requests.
NOTE: You MUST enter your IGN in the settings for this feature to work!

➤How to use:

✧Type "/who" in game lobby to get all players registered on overlay (might take a few secs). ✧Recommended: position overlay on top left or top right of screen and let the hands-free feature control overlay size (read more below)

➤Features:

✧Resizable by dragging the edges. ✧Hands-free: automatically shrinks to just the title bar when game starts, automatically unshrinks when there is a player change in lobby, you can toggle this mode with the green arrow on the top right

➤Session stats:

✧Click the clock button on the title bar to see your session stats, the changes in your stats since you opened the overlay. ✧Doing /api new will reset your session stats

➤Commands:

✧"/w cl" will clear the players on the overlay. ✧"/w ![IGN]" will show you the stats of the [IGN] of the player your entered (example: "/w !OhChit")

➤PS:

✧This overlay is in an EARLY PHASE, so expect bugs. It was made by Chit (OhChit). It's my first time making an app so please don't expect much. Please join the Discord and message me with any bugs or suggestions!
